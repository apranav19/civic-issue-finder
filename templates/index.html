Civic Issue Finder

Am embeddable widget to find issues in Civic Technology projects on the cfapi



Embed this to your Brigade site:

  1. Choose an organization, or leave blank to query all of the api.
  2. Enter a list of labels to always use when searching.
  3. Copy the following code somewhere in your website.
    <iframe src="http://localhost:5000/widget" width='300' height='600'> </iframe>
